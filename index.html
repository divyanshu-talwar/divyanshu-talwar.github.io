About me

Hi, I am Divyanshu Talwar, a curious skeptic intrigued by technology, mathematics, music, and nature.

I currently work as a software engineer at Google, focused on building and improving Google Cloud.
Before this, I served as a Vice President at Goldman Sachs, where I helped architect and build mechanisms to enhance the firm’s security, observability, and resilience.

My research interests span the areas of recommender systems, computer vision, and machine learning. You can explore my publications here or visit my Google Scholar profile.

I graduated Summa Cum Laude with a Bachelor's in Computer Science and Engineering from IIIT Delhi in 2019. During my time there, I had the privilege of collaborating with Dr. Angshul Majumdar, contributing to exciting advancements in recommender systems and machine learning. I also collaborated with Dr. Saket Anand on research related to representation learning and transfer learning in computer vision.

I’m always open to new conversations, collaborations, or opportunities. Feel free to reach out to me via email.
