About me

Hi, my name is Divyanshu Talwar. I am a curious skeptic intrigued by music, math, finance, and philosophy.

I currently work as an analyst in the Security Incident Response Team (SIRT) at Goldman Sachs, engineering solutions to effectively manage the firm’s technological risk.

My research interests span the areas of optimization, statistics, probability theory and machine learning. A list of my publications can be found here.

I graduated with a Bachelor’s in Computer Science and Engineering from IIIT-Delhi in 2019. During my time there I have had the good fortune of working with Dr. Angshul Majumdar on mathematically modeling collaborative filtering and bioinformatics problems and, with Dr. Saket Anand on exploring the area of representation learning (learning disentangled representations) along with its applications in zero/few-shot learning, transfer learning, and targetted data-augmentation.
