About me

Hi, I am Divyanshu Talwar, a curious skeptic intrigued by technology, mathematics, music, and nature.

I currently work as a software engineer at Goldman Sachs, where I architect and build mechanisms to enhance the firm’s observability and resilience.

My research interests span the areas of recommender systems, computer vision, and machine learning. You can explore my publications here or visit my Google Scholar profile.

I graduated Summa Cum Laude with a Bachelor's in Computer Science and Engineering from IIIT Delhi in 2019. During my time there, I had the privilege of collaborating with Dr. Angshul Majumdar, making impactful contributions in the domains of recommender systems and machine learning. I also worked under the guidance of Dr. Saket Anand, focusing on representation learning and transfer learning within computer vision.

Feel free to check out my resume or reach out to me via email. I’m always open to discussing potential collaborations or exploring new opportunities.
